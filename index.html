What kind of editorial assistance do I need?

  • Copywriting: Transform your ideas and research into copy.
  • Copyediting: Edit line by line for factual errors, grammatical errors, spelling, and word usage.
  • Proofreading: Check for small inconsistences, grammar mistakes, and typos before releasing your final product.
  • Editing: Provide suggestions for rewrites, organization, and other edits to improve the flow of your piece. This can include copyediting and proofreading.

Editorial

When I edit, I like to leave comments and suggestions. I find this to be better than just "fixing" things. My goal is for you, the writer, to understand my reasons for making a change. You can reject that change or learn from it. As for my suggestions, I hope that they guide you in deciding on the best way to say a particular phrase.
